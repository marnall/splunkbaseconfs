Have some down time between notable events? All caught up on your ES investigations? Why not relax and try to beat your high score?

Flappy Pony is the classic Flappy game with a Splunk twist. Guide the pony through the dangling ethernet cables and beat your personal best.


Controls
To move the pony up or down you can either click the left mouse button or press the SPACE key.

Sound
To mute or turn on music, press M
To mute or turn on sound effects, press S

Screen
To stretch the games screen to fill the window, press F
