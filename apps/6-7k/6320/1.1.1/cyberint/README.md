# Cyberint App for Splunk

Cyberint provides intelligence-driven digital risk protection. Cyberint helps you identify emerging threats, verifies your security posture, and responds effectively to reduce their impact.
Cyberint’s app is designed to bring enriched threat intelligence from the Argos Edge™ Digital Risk Protection Platform into Splunk.

This app provides you with the ability to:
- Leverage enriched and contextualized data from the Argos Edge™ Digital Risk Protection platform to maximize your security posture within Splunk.
- Easily consume targeted and actionable alerts of attack tools, phishing sites, fraud incidents, and more.
- Track the status of incidents with automatic sync updates across systems for instant visibility and comprehensive performance metrics.
For more information, visit our site at [cyberint.com](https://cyberint.com).
