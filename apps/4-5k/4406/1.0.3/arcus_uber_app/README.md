# Arcus Data Uber App

Visualize your Uber rider data using Splunk. The app ships dashboards that
slice and dice your personal Uber trip history — distance, fares, frequency,
geography, and ride patterns — using a sample CSV included with the app or
your own exported rider data.

For background and walkthroughs, see: https://blog.arcusdata.io/tag/uber
