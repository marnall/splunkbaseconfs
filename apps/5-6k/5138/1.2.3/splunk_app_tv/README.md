# Splunk App for TV

An app for enabling the full functionality of the Splunk TV suite of products, including Splunk TV for Apple, Android, and Fire TV, as well as Splunk TV Companion for iPad.

Please refer to [our documentation](https://docs.splunk.com/Documentation/TV) for more information about the product. If you have any unanswered questions or concerns after reading our documentation, please feel free to reach out directly to our team:

-	Benjamin Weaver (bweaver@splunk.com) - Engineering Manager
-	Kelly Kong (kkong@splunk.com) - Product Manager
