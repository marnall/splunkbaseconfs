The AlgoSec Splunk Incident Response App enhances and automates the security incident response process in the following ways:
•	AlgoSec highlights the potential impact on business applications and business processes.
•	AlgoSec also adds information regarding the infected server's exposure to the internet, or access to sensitive internal networks. This provides the security analyst with key information about the severity and urgency of the incident.
•	AlgoSec can also automate the action performed to contain the incident – such as automatically isolating the infected server from the network, in a single click!
