This Splunk app provides a collection of dashboards and alerts designed to surface 
meaningful and actionable data related to a Blue Prism environment. Information 
presented includes high level environmental data such as the installed application 
and database versions, installed license details, and listings of the installed 
environmental components (application servers, interactive clients, and runtime 
resources). Also included are views into digital worker sessions, configured 
work queues, and a searchable view of audit events.
