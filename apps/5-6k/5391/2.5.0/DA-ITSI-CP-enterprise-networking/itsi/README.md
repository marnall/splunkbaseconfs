## ITSI Content Pack for Cisco Enterprise Networks

The Content Pack for Cisco Enterprise Networks aggregates the data from Cisco Catalyst Center and Cisco Meraki to 
provide visibility into the health of networks. Its goal is to help identify the specific device(s) that are impacted 
for further troubleshooting and RCA.

For installation and configuration instructions see [the content pack documentation](https://quickdraw.splunk.com/redirect/?product=itsi&location=cp.cisco.enterprise.networks&version=current)

**Note**:  This content pack uses data from below Add-Ons:
* [Cisco Catalyst Add-on for Splunk](https://splunkbase.splunk.com/app/7538/)
* [Cisco Meraki Add-on for Splunk](https://splunkbase.splunk.com/app/5580/)
