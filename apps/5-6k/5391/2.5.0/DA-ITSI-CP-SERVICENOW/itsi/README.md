## ITSI Content Pack

The IT Service Intelligence (ITSI) Content Pack for ServiceNow is a collection of dashboards and their Knowledge Objects that gives you visibility into the health and performance of your ServiceNow environment, from Change Requests to CMDB and Dendrogram charts of service associations.
The dashboards leverage data collected using Splunk Add-on for ServiceNow.

For installation and configuration instructions, see [Install and configure the Content Pack for ServiceNow](https://docs.splunk.com/Documentation/CPServiceNow/latest/CP/Install).

**Note**: This content pack requires that you first install and Configure the [Splunk Add-on for ServiceNow](https://splunkbase.splunk.com/app/1928/) and the [Dendrogram visualization](https://splunkbase.splunk.com/app/5153).
