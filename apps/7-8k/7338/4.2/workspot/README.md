### PURPOSE:

The Workspot Splunk Add-on allows enterprises to retrieve their critical data from Workspot service for their analyses. You can consume the data by downloading the application from your Workspot Control subscription and entering the security credentials displayed in Control.

 

### PREREQUISITES:

####Configuring Splunk : \
Ensure Workspot Splunk Add-on is enabled on your Workspot tenent. \
Installation instructions are provided here:

https://community.workspot.com/splunk-89/workspot-configuration-guide-for-splunk-349
