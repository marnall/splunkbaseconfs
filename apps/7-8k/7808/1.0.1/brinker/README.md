# Brinker Splunk App

Brinker is an end-to-end disinformation threat mitigation platform designed to combat digital malicious narratives and foreign influence campaigns through proprietary narrative intelligence technology. The SaaS platform offers AI-powered detection, context analysis, and automated OSINT investigation. A suite of mitigation tools is available at the press of a button, including pre-legal action, media publication, content removal, and counter-narratives. Brinker is a Mastercard portfolio company. Its client base includes governmental intelligence institutions, major enterprises, law firms, and NGOs.

## Overview

This Splunk app serves as an informational entry point to the Brinker ecosystem from within the Splunk environment.

## Features

- Provides a branded experience inside Splunk
- Offers quick access to the broader Brinker platform

## Getting Started

1. Install the app on your Splunk instance
2. Click on the Brinker icon in the app menu
3. Explore available tools and insights

## Additional Information

- This app does not collect data or run Splunk searches
- No configuration is required after installation
