# About

KELA, the global leader of Dark Net threat intelligence, monitors the Dark Net to provide government agencies and enterprises with cyber threats targeting their organizations, employees, supply chains, and more. KELA's RaDark is a fully automated, scalable, and manageable targeted threat intelligence platform, enabling users to gain full visibility into their organization's exposure as seen by cybercriminals. By continuously monitoring the organization's attack surface as well as chatter around relevant assets in the cybercrime financial ecosystem, RaDark allows defenders to remediate threats and mitigate vulnerabilities before they turn into a more severe cyber attack. KELA's solutions are leveraged by cyber security teams globally for various purposes, such as:
brand protection
vulnerability management
security operations
corporate security
supply chain management
insider threat detection
executive protection
and more.
