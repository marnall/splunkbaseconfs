Initial preview release of Detecteam app for Splunk. Queries, detections, dashboards, collections and detection scoring. Detecteam descriptive statistics, and scoring. contact splunk (at) detecteam (dot) com for access to the Detecteam platform

Dashboards
Detecteam Executive Summary - shares the shape of the detection ecosystem in Splunk focused on accuracy, coverage and lineage
Detecteam Data Overview - Time-based approach the the operations and execution of Detecteam scenarios, by sourcetype distribution, time and impact on Splunk deployment.
Splunk Environment Descriptives - the shape and distribution of data by sourcetypes, sources, indexes and statistics used for data simulation accuracy
Detecteam Scenario Analysis - focused on evaluating Detections against Detecteam scenarios, available data, configuration, trigger rate and available detections by Detecteam scenario.
Detecteam Scenario Drilldown - a worflow-based approach to investigate the data for any given scenario, generated by Detecteam, and the implications of what types of detections are related to the data.
Scenario to Detection Baseline Behavior - a graphical represenation of the triggered / available / enabled detections by MITRE ATT&CK labels, based on all detecteam scenarios.
Splunk Analytic Story Coverage - a relation comparison to Splunk Analytic stories for coverage and gaps, as well as configuration or enabling with the lineage of where the data and detection come from.  includes detection data coverage and triggered detection activities.
