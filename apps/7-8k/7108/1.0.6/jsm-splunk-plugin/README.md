
## App Description

Overview:

Jira Service Management is an alerting, on-call management and incident response orchestration solution that ensures faster repair and resolution. Trusted by development and operations teams in over 2500 organizations worldwide, Jira Service Management provides the tools needed to design actionable alerts, manage on-call schedules & escalations, and orchestrate communication & collaboration during incident resolution process. Learn more at https://www.Jira Service Management.com.

If the feature isn't available on your site, keep checking Jira Service Management for updates

What does Jira Service Management offer to Splunk users?
Jira Service Management provides a powerful integration with Splunk.
When an alert is created in Splunk, the alert is created in Jira Service Management automatically.
Jira Service Management can filter, append and format the information, providing more actionable data about the alert.
Jira Service Management will then spawn notifications based on on-call schedules and escalation policies.
To learn more about Jira Service Management's Splunk app, please refer to the documentation at https://docs.Jira Service Management.com/docs/splunk-integration.


## Installation Instructions

* Install via the Splunk Web Admin
* Create an Alert configuration, select Jira Service Management as the trigger action.
* Paste the URL provided by Jira Service Management while saving the integration in Jira Service Management side.
