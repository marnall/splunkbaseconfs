XposedOrNot Splunk App Version 1.0.0

Description

The XposedOrNot Splunk App integrates seamlessly with the XposedOrNot platform to provide comprehensive insights into data breaches and exposed email information. By collecting data from the XposedOrNot API, the app offers real-time updates on breached and exposed emails associated with verified domains. It effectively aggregates and visualizes this critical information, enabling users to monitor and evaluate their domain security status.

In addition to its powerful visualization features, the app includes two key alerts: "New Data Breach Alert" and "New Data Breach Alert (Email)." When triggered, these alerts automatically create a notable event within the Splunk Enterprise Security app, allowing for immediate response to potential threats.

The XposedOrNot Splunk App enhances your organization's ability to respond quickly to security risks, providing instant access to detailed breach and exposure data, helping you to protect your domain proactively.
