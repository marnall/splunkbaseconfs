###### About  ######
This Techncal Add-on Maps Zscaler log data to Splunk CIM.  For full doc please visit https://help.zscaler.com/zia/nss-configuration-example-splunk


###### Support ######
The Zscaler Splunk App is supported by the Zscaler Team: all requests for support should be directed to: splunk-support@zscaler.com
