ZeroFOX for Splunk

ZeroFOX continuously monitors social media and digital channels for security
threats and business risks. The ZeroFOX for Splunk app enables organizations
to visualize and analyze these threats directly from the purpose-built
Splunk App. Integrating ZeroFOX alerts and intelligence into Splunk gives
users a new way to address the dynamically changing social threat vector
and improve security posture through correlation with other internal IT
and security data sources. 
    
    - Identify and visualize impersonations, fraud, phishing, malware and
      sensitive data leakage on social networks directly in Splunk.
    - Inject social media threat visibility into your security environment,
      powering correlation of suspicious activity and providing social context
      for network anomalies.
    - Track and report on overall social risk posture and security trends over
      time via pre-built graphical reports.


Getting Started:

    1) Download and install the ZeroFOX for Splunk app
    2) Login with your ZeroFOX Platform account (via Data Inputs)
    3) If you do not have a ZeroFOX Platform account or have exceeded your
       maximum number of app connectors, contact a Solutions Architect.
