Description
-----------------------------------------
The ThreatConnect® App for Splunk gives Splunk users the ability to
leverage customizable threat intelligence integrated into Splunk from their
ThreatConnect accounts.  ThreatConnect provides the ability to aggregate threat
intelligence from multiple sources (open source, commercial, communities, and
internally created), analyze and track identified adversary infrastructure and
capabilities, and put that refined knowledge to work in Splunk identifying
threats targeting your organization.
