A10-SSLi_Splunk_App installation guidlines.


1)Log into Splunk Enterprise.
2)On the Apps menu, click Manage Apps.
3)Click Install app from file.
4)In the Upload app window, click Choose File.
5)Locate the .tar.gz file you just downloaded, and then click Open or Choose.
6)Click Upload.

7)After successful installation of app(A10-SSLi_Splunk_App) goto SPLUNK_HOME/etc/apps/A10-SSLi_Splunk_App/appserver/addons
8)Copy all folders(custom_double_donut_chart, sankey_diagram_app, variation_Chart) from addons directory and paste them to SPLUNK_HOME/etc/apps/
9)Restart Splunk.
