# Publishing Checklist

Before publishing to Splunkbase, update the following:
- Author and support contact in app.conf and app.manifest.
- License text in LICENSE.txt and license metadata in app.manifest.
- Privacy policy URL (if required) in app.manifest.
- Splunkbase listing details (categories, screenshots, support level).
- Validate with AppInspect (precert) on Linux to clear OS-specific warnings.
