Welcome to the Splunk for Bluecoat Application


Loading ProxySG Data The appp relies on the sourcetype=bcoat_proxysg and uses the following field extraction by default.

[delimExtractions]
DELIMS=" " FIELDS="date","time","time_taken","dvc_ip","user","user_group","x_exception_id","filter_result","category","http_referrer","holder","http_response","action","http_method","http_content_type","uri_scheme","dest_host","dest_port","uri_path","uri_query","uri_extension","http_user_agent","src_ip","sc_bytes","cs_bytes","x_virus_id"
updates to this extraction should be done in $SPLUNK_HOME/etc/apps/SplunkforBluecoat/local/transforms.conf
Loading CacheFlow data The app relies on the sourcetype bcoat_cacheflow for the CacheFlow data
The dashboards are driven off of the search BlueCoat - DataCube.
With an Splunk Enterprise License this search will run every 6 hours updating a cache. You may change this frequency by editing the search under the manger.
